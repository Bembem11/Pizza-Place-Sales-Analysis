# Pizza-Place-Sales-Analysis
This analysis explores a yearâ€™s sales data from a pizza restaurant to understand revenue performance, customer demand, and sales trends across different time periods and product categories.

## Tools used
- Language: Python
- Libaries: Pandas (Data Manipulation), Numpy (Numerical Analysis) , Matplotlib (Data Visualization)

## Datasets Used for Analysis
This project analysis uses four CSV files containing one year of sales data.
- Orders: Contains the `order_id`, date, and time for every transaction.
- Order Details: Tracks the specific `pizza_id` and quantity for each order.
- Pizzas: Provides the price and size for each pizza variant.
- Pizza Types: Includes the name, category, and ingredients for each pizza.

## Key Findings
### 1. Total Revenue
The total revenue generated from pizza place sales is **81,7860.05**
### 2. Total Quantity Sold
A total of **49,574** pizzas were sold during the year, indicating strong customer demand.
### 3. Total Orders
The restaurant processed **21,350** orders during the year, showing high level of customer traffic.
### 4. Pizza Types
The restaurant offers **32** different pizza types, providing customers with a wide variety of options.
### 5. Average Price
The average price of pizzas is **16.49** suggesting that the restaurant targets a budget-friendly market.
### 6. Peak Sales Hours
Sales peak around **12:00 - 13:00** and **17:00 - 19:00**, indicating that customers mostly order during lunch and dinner hours.
### 7. Sales by Day of Week
The highest sales occur on **Fridays**, likely due to increased customer activity on that day. Thursdays and Fridays are the busiest days of the week.
### 8. Top 5 Bestselling Pizzas
The most popular pizzas are:
- The Classic Deluxe Pizza      
- The Barbecue Chicken Pizza    
- The Hawaiian Pizza           
- The Pepperoni Pizza          
- The Thai Chicken Pizza
   
These pizzas contribute significantly to the total revenue.
### 9. Monthly Sales Trend
Sales flunctates throughout the year, peaking in July with **72,557.90** and reaching lowest sales in October with **64,027.60**
Mid-year recorded the highest sales, while September-October had significant decline.
### 10. Low-Performing Pizzas
Some pizza types recorded low sales, **The Brie Carre Pizza** is the lowest-performing item in both quantity and revenue.
suggesting they may need menu improvement or promotion.

## Recommendation
### 1. Optimize Staffing During Peak Hours and Days
- Since  pizza sales surge at peak periods, the restaurant should schedule more staff during lunch and dinner rush to improve service speed and customer satisfaction. And also during peak days Thursdays and Fridays.
- The Pizza PLace can also have prepare ingredients in advance to reduce waiting time, they can maintain a high order completion rate during peak demands.
  
### 2. Promote Sales on Low-Performing Pizzas 
There are few pizzas with low such as **Brie Carre Pizza**
- Offer discounts to test demand from the customers
- Consider revising the recipes or ingredients of these pizzas
- Combo deals with bestselling pizzas at discounted prices can also help boost the sales.
- Removing these pizzas if their performance does not improve

### 3. Boost Sales in Low Perfoming Month
There is decline in sales from September to October, imploring some measures:
- Introducing limited-time flavors using like the top bestseller ingredients to launch **Fall Flavour**
- Run targeted promotions campaigns in October to help with the sales
  
 This helps stabilize revenue throughout this period and overall the year.  

### 4. Promoting Bestselling Pizzas Heavily
- The Top performing pizzas should be featured on the front of the menu, highlighted in advertisements.
- Loyalty card can be introduced specially for these 5 types, the more you buys, the loyalty points you earn. The cutomers can get discounts with these points.
  
This encourages them buying more pizzas and ensures they keep coming back for what you already do best.
  
### 5. Expand Menu Based on Customer Preferences
The restaurant already offers 32 pizza types and based on the analysis on the category, New menu can be curate to introduce more varieties in high-demand categories and remove rarely ordered categories.
